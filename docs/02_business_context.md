# Business Context

This project simulates a fashion retail company with multiple stores, brands, products, purchase orders, transfer orders, and weekly forecasts.

## Business Questions

### Sales

- Which stores have the highest sales?
- Which items are best sellers?
- Which brands are underperforming?

### Inventory

- Which stores have excess stock?
- Which items have low stock?
- What is the total stock value?

### Purchase Orders

- Which purchase orders are still open?
- Which vendors have pending quantities?
- What is the value of remaining purchase orders?

### Transfers

- Which transfers are in transit?
- Which transfers have short or excess received quantity?
- Which stores are waiting for stock?

### Forecast

- How accurate was the forecast?
- Is the business over-forecasting or under-forecasting?
- Which items have high forecast error?
